Lamb Steak

Grilled lamb steaks are a flavorful and hearty dish known for their rich, tender texture and distinctive savory taste. Marinated with herbs, garlic, and seasonings, the lamb develops a delicious depth of flavor that is enhanced by the smoky char from the grill. The outside becomes beautifully caramelized while the inside remains juicy and tender.

This dish is perfect for family dinners, backyard cookouts, or special occasions. Served alongside roasted vegetables, rice, potatoes, or a fresh salad, grilled lamb steaks offer a satisfying meal that combines bold flavors with simple preparation. The balance of herbs, spices, and grilled meat creates an unforgettable dining experience.

Ingredients

  • 4 lamb steaks
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  2. Rub the marinade evenly over both sides of the lamb steaks.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 4 hours for more flavor.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Place the lamb steaks on the grill and cook for 4–6 minutes per side, depending on thickness and desired doneness.
  6. Remove the steaks from the grill and let them rest for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite side dishes and enjoy.
